Help Papa send the undead back into the ground where they belong!

The s&box version of Grave Quest was originally made in 2021 but ended up breaking due to a major engine update. It was recreated entirely (again) about 3 years later with new features and polish.

It features leaderboards, new music, sound effects, player customization and more.

You can also check out the pages for the UE5 version, or the original Unity version.
