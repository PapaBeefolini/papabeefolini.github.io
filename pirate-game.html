Open world procedurally generated co-op survival adventure pirate game.

Pirate game is a rough and early attempt at making a co-op procedural adventure game. Sail around islands with your crew collecting treasure and booty.

A good amount of work went into the boat physics. Players can freely and smoothly move around the ship while the ship itself moves across the ocean, without crazy physics glitches that fling the player overboard.
