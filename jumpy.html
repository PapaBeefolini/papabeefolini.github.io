Dodge cars & jump to victory as you race your friends across a procedural & vibrant world!

Jumpy is a multiplayer Frogger clone with a procedurally generated world, utilizing the s&box engine and later ported to Unity for web.

The s&box version was made in about a month, and then the Unity web version was made in around 5 days.
