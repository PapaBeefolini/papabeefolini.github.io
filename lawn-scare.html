Do yard work and drink beer. - You're tasked with completing chores around your backyard, from watering the flowers, to leaf-blowing and mowing. There's plenty of work to be done before the wife gets home! Features a unique art-style inspired by crappy ms-paint doodles and shows like Ed-Edd-n-Eddy.
