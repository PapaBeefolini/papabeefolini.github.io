Washed Ashore is a multiplayer re-creation of Stranded 2. With new features and mechanics.

With permission to use the assets of the original Stranded 2 creator, I set out on one of my first attempts at a networked multiplayer survival game with procedurally generated islands. It was in development for around 3 months before I finally decided to move on to other projects.

A decent amount of the game was completed and even had an IndieDB page created for it, but ultimately just ended up getting scrapped in favor of more original content.
